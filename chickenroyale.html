Chicken Royale Recipe

Recipe Description

  • Perfect for one serving.
  • A declicious main meal.
  • Not suitable for Vegetarians. See our other recipes for Vegetarian options!

Ingredients

  • Seeded Rolls
  • Battered Chicken Steak
  • Mayonnaise

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C/Fan 180°C/Gas 6.
  2. Remove the chicken from its packaging and place on a baking tray in the centre of the oven for 30 minutes.
  3. Slice the bread rolls and apply a generous layer of mayonnaise to one side. Add any desired salad such as Romaine lettuce.
  4. Once cooked, add the chicken breast to the roll. Allow to cool slightly then enjoy with an ice-cold beverage.
