The Impossible Whopper

Description of recipe

  • Servings: 4
  • Main Course
  • Vegan

Ingredients

  • 4 sesame buns
  • 3/4 teaspoon liquid smoke
  • 1 pound of ground Impossible protein
  • 12 dill pickle slices
  • 8 slices red onion
  • 8 slices tomato
  • 8 hand torn leaves of iceberg lettuce
  • 4 tablespoons vegan mayonnaise
  • salt & pepper to taste

Method

  1. Mix ground protein and liquid smoke together in a bowl.
  2. SDivide mixture into 4 equal portions and press each in a burger press or with your hands. When shaping the mixture, press hard, or the patty wonâ€™t hold together when cooking.
  3. Cook each patty on a pan for about 4 minutes on each side. You can cook the patty in the oven at 375 degrees Fahrenheit (190 degrees Celsius) to cook fully.
  4. Once the patty is done, assemble the burger.
  5. Take the lettuce leaves and wash them if needed, cut the tomato and pickle into discs, and slice the onion into rings.
  6. Place down the bottom bun, then the patty, pickle slices, onion rings, tomato slices, lettuce leaves, the vegan mayonnaise, and finally the top bun.
  7. Try and enjoy!
