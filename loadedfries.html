Recipe Description

  • Perfect for one serving.
  • A delicious side.
  • Suitable for vegetarians!

Ingredients

  • Chips or Fries
  • Cheese, any type depending on your preference.
  • Sauce! Get creative! Mayo, BBQ, Ketchup, Perinaise.

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C/Fan 180°C/Gas 6.
  2. Place the chips in an ovenproof dish and cook for 10 minutes until the chips are lightly cooked.
  3. Add your sauce and any additional toppings such as chilli, parsley, garlic, onions or meats. Return to the oven for a further 10 minutes.
  4. Remove from the oven carefully and rest for 2 minutes as the dish will be very hot. Enjoy alongside an ice-cold beverage!
